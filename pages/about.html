ABOUT

“To dare is to lose one's footing momentarily. Not to dare is to lose oneself.” - Soren Kierkegaard

Starting in 2019, we've taken our lenses throughout the world. Cities capture our eyes while vast landscapes charm our hearts. Throughout each unthought and whimsical adventure, we find where we are supposed to be. In beautiful places, our cameras find the wonderous memories of the world.
